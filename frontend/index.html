Capabilities

Everything an inspector needs,
automated by AI

⬡

PDF Text Extraction

Full-page text extracted in reading order using PyMuPDF. Handles multi-column layouts, damaged PDFs, and mixed encodings.

⬡

Thermal Analysis

Parses thermal imaging reports to identify temperature anomalies, moisture signatures, and insulation failures.

⬡

Image Extraction

Pulls all embedded images, deduplicates them by hash, and associates each with its source page for evidence linking.

⬡

Conflict Detection

Compares inspection and thermal findings. Flags when reports contradict — e.g. "no moisture" vs. "thermal anomaly detected."

⬡

Duplicate Merging

Identifies semantically equivalent observations across both reports and consolidates them into a single authoritative finding.

⬡

Severity Assessment

Each observation is classified Critical / High / Medium / Low based on structural risk, safety impact, and urgency.

⬡

Root Cause Analysis

Gemini infers probable causes from the evidence — pointing to construction defects, material failure, or deferred maintenance.

⬡

Professional PDF Generation

Outputs a branded, paginated DDR with cover page, executive summary, embedded images, and numbered recommendations.

Process

From two PDFs to a complete diagnostic report

01

Upload Reports

Drag and drop your Inspection PDF and Thermal PDF. Files are validated and securely processed.

02

Extract & Parse

PyMuPDF extracts all text and images from both PDFs, preserving page structure and evidence associations.

03

AI Analysis

Gemini receives both reports and runs structured analysis: merging duplicates, flagging conflicts, scoring severity.

04

Download DDR

A professional PDF DDR is generated with all findings, images, and recommendations ready for client delivery.

About

Built for property inspectors and diagnostic professionals

DDR AI Generator replaces the manual process of cross-referencing inspection and thermal reports. Instead of hours spent comparing findings, spotting contradictions, and writing summaries — this system does it in under a minute.

The output is a structured, client-ready Detailed Diagnostic Report that holds up to professional scrutiny: every finding sourced, every conflict documented, every recommendation specific and actionable.
